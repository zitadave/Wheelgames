## 🎮 The Games: Detailed Overview

### 1. Even/Odd Wheel of Fortune (ቀይ እና ሰማያዊ)
*   **The Concept:** A classic, fast-paced multiplier wheel game.
*   **How to Play:**
    1.  Select your desired bet amount.
    2.  Place your prediction on either **Even** (Red) or **Odd** (Blue) sectors of the spinning wheel.
    3.  A real-time countdown leads to the wheel spin accompanied by responsive haptic ticks and sound effects.
    4.  If the selector lands on your predicted sector, you double your bet instantly! Landing on special multiplier slots can amplify rewards even further.
*   **Key Features:** Synchronized multiplayer countdowns, authentic mechanical wheel animations, and real-time audio feedback.

### 2. Jackpot Arena (የጃክፖት አሬና)
*   **The Concept:** A competitive multiplayer prize-pool arena where your winning probability scales dynamically with your contribution.
*   **How to Play:**
    1.  Enter the active round by adding your desired amount to the cumulative Jackpot pool.
    2.  Your slice of the circular progress arena represents your winning probability (e.g., if you contribute 50% of the total pool, you own a 50% winning chance).
    3.  Once the round timer expires, a dramatic spinning odometer and ticket sector animation starts.
    4.  The needle selects a single winner who walks away with the entire collective pool (minus a small house fee)!
*   **Key Features:** Live-updating circular charts showing real-time player shares, high-stakes theater mode, and dramatic win announcements.

### 3. Wheel of Chance (የዕድል መንኮራኩር)
*   **The Concept:** A personalized, instant-win multiplier wheel where every spin guarantees a return multiplier.
*   **How to Play:**
    1.  Configure your instant-spin stake.
    2.  Trigger the spin; watch the wheel accelerate with fluid momentum.
    3.  The wheel settles on designated multiplier sectors ranging from `0.1x` up to huge jackpot multipliers like `10x` or `50x`.
    4.  Winnings are credited immediately to your digital wallet.
*   **Key Features:** High-resolution vector-rendered wheel canvas, customizable volatility, and instant local state balancing.

### 4. Multiplayer Bingo (ቢንጎ አዳራሽ)
*   **The Concept:** A legendary Ethiopian social game digitized into a real-time multiplayer card daubing experience with an automatic Amharic voice caller!
*   **How to Play:**
    1.  Join an active Bingo lobby and purchase one or more cards featuring a grid of numbers.
    2.  As the game starts, a live ball dispenser drops numbered balls one by one.
    3.  Our **Amharic Voice Caller Engine** reads out the numbers aloud in real-time (e.g., *"ቁጥር ሰባት... ቁጥር ሰባ አምስት..."*).
    4.  Select *Auto-Daub* to have the system mark your cards, or manually tap numbers to race other players.
    5.  The first player to complete a winning line or a full house pattern triggers **"BINGO!"** to secure the community prize pool.
*   **Key Features:** Native voice synthesizer, multi-card support, and persistent real-time lobby state sync.
